Worldcoin’s new Orb Mini looks like a piece of Apple hardware with a God complex. You hold it up to your face. It scans your iris. Then it gives you crypto—and a badge that says, “you’re human now.”

It looks absurd.
It is.
But it’s also worse than it looks.

Red Flags#

  • Banned or investigated in Spain, Portugal, Kenya
  • Reports of scanning minors
  • Core systems remain closed

The Altman Stack#

Sam Altman (Worldcoin, OpenAI) now partners with Anduril, makers of military drone AI.
This isn’t just crypto hype. It’s a surveillance pipeline.

Try Better#

Holonym and PrivID offer cryptographic alternatives.
Open-source. No biometric honeypots.

Worldcoin isn’t the future. It’s a warning.
